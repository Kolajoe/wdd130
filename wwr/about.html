History

Founded in 2025 by passionate river guides, Wild Waters Rafting started as a small operation with just two rafts and a dream. Over the years, we have grown into one of the most trusted rafting companies in the region, guiding thousands of adventurers down the most exciting rivers. Our commitment to safety, environmental stewardship, and exceptional customer service has made us a favorite among both beginners and experienced rafters. Today, we continue to explore new rivers and share our love for white water adventure with people from all over the world.
