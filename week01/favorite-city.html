Welcome to Kinshasa

The vibrant capital of the DRC

Kinshasa is a fascinating city located on the banks of the Congo River. It is the third largest city in Africa and it buzzes with energy day and night. What I particularly like about Kinshasa is its unique atmosphere, its lively music, and the warmth of its inhabitants.

The city is famous for its music scene with Congolese rumba, its dances like ndombolo, and its talented artists. The central market is a colorful place where you can find everything, while the Gombe is the modern district with its skyscrapers.

Some specialties of Kinshasa:

  • Music - Papa Wemba, Koffi Olomidé, Fally Ipupa
  • Gastronomy - fufu, pondu, skewers
  • The Congo River - the second most powerful river in the world
  • Resourcefulness and 'Article 15'
