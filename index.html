Kinshasa

Kinshasa is the capital of the Democratic Republic of the Congo. It borders the Congo River and is the neighboring city to Brazzaville, which is the capital of the Republic of the Congo; the two cities are therefore the closest capitals and are located on either side of the river. Kinshasa is the financial and cultural center of the country and hosts the entire administration of the Democratic Republic of the Congo. The city is home to the legendary Martyrs Stadium, the People's Palace, the cultural center, and the Nsele Valley Park. Kinshasa is better known for its musical and cultural activities, with some even considering it the capital of African music.
